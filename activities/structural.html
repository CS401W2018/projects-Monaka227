QUestions and Answers

1. What goes in the header?

Header tag contains introductory content or navigation links, such as the website’s logo, title, or a top navigation menu.


2. What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

Nav tag contains the website's main navigation, usually links to internal pages. It can go in the header but doesn’t have to. It’s often placed there for convenience. The nav can contain links to both internal and external websites.


3. What goes in Main?

Main tag contains the primary content of the webpage, such as articles, text, images, or other relevant elements.

4. What goes in the Footer?

Footer tag contains information such as copyright, contact details, privacy policy, or additional navigation links.
