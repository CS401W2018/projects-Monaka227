My Hero - Rui Hachimura

Rui Hachimura is an NBA player from Japan, currently playing for the Los Angeles Lakers. With a strong physicality and high basketball IQ, he plays both offensively and defensively as a forward. He first came to prominence at Gonzaga University and joined the Washington Wizards as a first-round pick in the 2019 NBA Draft. He is the first Japanese player to achieve success in the NBA and has made a name for himself around the world.
