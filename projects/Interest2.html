My Interest - My favorite NBA Teams

Los Angels Lakers

The Los Angeles Lakers are one of the most popular and historic teams in the NBA, winning many championships. The team is especially famous for its star players such as Magic Johnson, Kobe Bryant, and LeBron James. The Lakers are also closely tied to the glamorous Los Angeles entertainment culture.

Boston Celtics

The Boston Celtics are the league's all-time winningest team. They are known for their fierce defense and teamwork, featuring legends such as Bill Russell and Larry Bird. Their rivalry with the Lakers is one of the most famous in NBA history.

Golden State Warriors

The Golden State Warriors are a team that has been particularly hot in the NBA in recent years, having found great success in the 2010s with their 3-point shooting-heavy style led by Steph Curry. The team used to be based in Oakland, but has now relocated to San Francisco. The Warriors attract fans with their speedy and exciting style of play.
