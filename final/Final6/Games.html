Popular Games

On this page you will find several board games. The rules of these games are also briefly explained, but for more detailed information, please refer to the official websites or Board Game Arana.

Title: Catan (originally The Settlers of Catan)
Awards: Spiel des Jahres (1995), Deutscher Spiele Preis (1995), Origins Award (1996), and many others
Relese Year: 1995
Number of Players: 3-4(base game), up to 6 with expansion
Brief Rules: Players are settlers on an island, collecting resources to build settlements, cities, and roads. The goal is to reach 10 victory points through building and special achievements.
Expansions: 5-6 players expansion, Seafarers, Traders & Barbarians and others.

Title: Terraforming Mars
Awards: German Game Prize (2017)
Relese Year: 2016
Number of Players: 1-5 players.
Brief Rules: Players become CEOs of separate companies with different personalities and develop Mars. The player who contributes more to the terraforming of Mars wins.
Expansions: Venus Next, Prelude, Prelude2 and others.

Title: Ticket to Ride
Awards: 2004 Spiel des Jahres (Game of the Year), Deutscher Spiel Preis (6th place) and many other.
Relese Year: 2004
Number of Players: 2-5 players.
Brief Rules: The objective of the game is to connect cities using train routes. Players earn points by completing routes between cities indicated on their destination cards. Each player has 45 train pieces in their chosen color and collects train cards to claim routes. The game ends when a player has two or fewer train pieces left. The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins.
Expansions: The basic version is North America, but there are also expansions for Europe, Germany, London, Italy, Japan, etc.

Title: AZUL
Awards:
Relese Year: 2017
Number of Players: 2-4 players.
Brief Rules: Players collect and place tiles that decorate the walls of a Portuguese royal palace. The game consists of several rounds, and in each round players perform the following actions: take all tiles of the same color from the factory and place the rest in the center. Place the tiles taken on the pattern lines on his/her player board. At the end of the round, the completed line of tiles is moved to the wall and the score is calculated. The game ends when someone completes a horizontal line of walls and the player with the highest score wins.
Expansions: Stained Glass of Sintra, Summer Pavilion, Queen's Garden.

Title: Legends of Andor
Awards: 2013 Kennerspiel des Jahres (Connoisseur Game of the Year)
Relese Year: 2012
Number of Players: 2-4 players
Brief Rules: This is a cooperative adventure board game where players take on the roles of heroes defending the kingdom of Andor from invading forces. The game is played over a series of scenarios called "Legends," each with specific objectives and storylines. Players must work together to complete objectives while managing resources and battling enemies. The game features a time track that dictates the progression of events, adding strategic depth as players must balance completing tasks with managing threats.
Expansions: The Star Shield, Journey to the North, The Last Hope, New Heroes

Title: Battle Line
Awards: No awards.
Relese Year: 2000
Number of Players: 2 players
Brief Rules: Players face off across a 'battle line' with 9 flags.The goal is to win by capturing 5 of 9 flags or 3 adjacent flags.Players create 3-card poker-like formations on either side of each flag.The highest formation wins the flag. Tactics cards can be played, with a rule that you can only play one more tactics card than your opponent.
Expansions: No expansions.

Title: Codenames
Awards: Nominated for 27 awards and won 15 in total
Relese Year: 2015
Number of Players: 2-8 players
Brief Rules: Two teams, led by a spymaster who knows the identities of 25 secret agents, will play against each other. Each secret agent is assigned one word. The spymaster gives a one-word clue and indicates the number of cards associated with that clue by a number. Team members guess which agent belongs to their team based on these clues. The first team to correctly identify all the agents wins.
Expansions: Codenames Pictures, Codenames Duet.
