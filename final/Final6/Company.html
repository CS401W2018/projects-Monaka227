Major Board Game Production Companies

This page summarizes several major board game production companies. Please use this page as a reference to find board games you like, as similar games are often released by the same production company.

1.Asmodee Editions

Notable Titles: Ticket to Ride, Pandemic, 7 Wonders
History: Founded in 1995 in France by Marc Nunes, Asmodee has become a major player in the board game industry through its strategy of acquiring other game publishers like Fantasy Flight Games and Days of Wonder. The company is known for its high-quality productions and innovative designs.
Scale: Over 2,200 employees globally, operations in Europe, North America, South America, and Asia.
Mission: To unite people through great games and amazing stories.
Target: Wide range, from experienced gamers to newcomers, solo players to large groups.

2.Hasbro

Notable Titles: Monopoly, Trivial Pursuit, Dungeons & Dragons
History: Established in 1923 by the Hassenfeld brothers in the United States, Hasbro is one of the largest toy companies worldwide. It has expanded its board game portfolio by acquiring well-known brands such as Parker Brothers and Wizards of the Coast.
Scale: One of the largest global toy companies.
Mission: While not explicitly stated, focuses on expanding global business using a wide board game portfolio and strengthening presence in media and entertainment industries.
Target: Broad age range

3.Ravensburger

Notable Titles: Labyrinth, Scotland Yard
History: Founded in 1883 in Germany by Otto Robert Maier, Ravensburger is renowned for its puzzles and educational games. It has a strong reputation for quality craftsmanship and has sold millions of units of its popular board games like Labyrinth.
Scale: Headquartered in Germany with subsidiaries in major Western European countries
MissionProvide high-quality jigsaw puzzles and board games
Target: Strong focus on family-oriented games

4.Goliath Games

Notable Titles: Rummikub, Triominos, Pop the Pig
History: Established in 1980 by Adi Golad in the Netherlands, Goliath Games has grown into one of the largest toy companies globally. The company markets a wide range of games and toys across more than 70 countries.
Scale: One of the world's largest toy companies, exports products to over 70 countries.
MissionWhile not explicitly stated, focuses on global expansion and offering diverse product lines.
Target: Wide age range.

5.FryxGames

Notable Titles: Terraforming Mars
History: FryxGames was founded by the Fryxelius family in Sweden. The company is unique as it is a family-run business, with several members of the Fryxelius family contributing to game design and development.
Scale: Family-run board game publisher based in Sweden
MissionWhile not explicitly stated, focuses on creating high-quality and innovative games
Target: Players who enjoy strategic and complex games
