What is a Board Games?

Board game is a general term for games played on a table using tools such as pieces, cards, dice, and paper. They are also called analog games, and are characterized by the fact that they do not use computers or game consoles.﻿Board games require a variety of elements depending on the game, such as tact, foresight, deduction, luck, speed, and balance, and the complex interplay of these elements creates interesting and deep gameplay. Typical board games include Shogi, Igo, Othello, the Game of Life, and Monopoly.
